How it works

No app. No login. Support that simply arrives.

The organisation signs people up. Each participant gets instant access through a channel they already use every day. Nothing to download, no account to create.

The biggest difference

The two parts

A guided series, and a dietitian who never clocks off.

Part one

A structured video series

Six weeks and eighteen short videos, built and presented by registered dietitians. Small changes, paced so they actually stick.
Part two

An AI dietitian, on call

Everyday questions answered in the moment, in the participant's own language and at their own level. Twelve months of access, so support lasts well past the series.

Clear scope, by design

Lifestyle and nutrition education only. The service never diagnoses; any medical question is referred straight back to the participant's own doctor or dietitian. Not a medical device.
