The problem & our mission

We exist for the people standard programmes overlook.

Happy Dietitian is not-just-for-profit. We started because we saw a gap others step over: people with low health literacy and lower incomes are the hardest to reach and the ones most health programmes consistently miss.

That gap has a cost. People in lower socio-economic groups live on average 8 years shorter and spend around 23 years more of their lives in poorer health. Closing it is the whole reason we are here.
