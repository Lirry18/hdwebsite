Not-just-for-profit

We exist because we found a gap others step over.

Standard health programmes reach the people who are already engaged, already informed, already well. The people they consistently miss are those with low health literacy and lower incomes — the people who need support most.

We're not-just-for-profit. We didn't start with a product looking for a market — we started with a gap, and built the simplest thing that could close it. Reaching those people is the whole point, not a side effect.
